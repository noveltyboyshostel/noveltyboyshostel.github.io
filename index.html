About Us

Welcome to Sunshine Hostel! We provide a friendly and budget-friendly accommodation for travelers from all over the world. Our common areas are perfect for meeting new people and sharing stories. Located just a 5-minute walk from the central station and main attractions.

Our mission is to make your stay comfortable, fun, and memorable. We offer free Wi-Fi, breakfast, and a tour desk to help you explore the city.
